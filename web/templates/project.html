Uncertainty methods

Four methods are evaluated for their ability to produce calibrated, useful uncertainty estimates on a ViT-based histopathology classifier.

  • Confidence (1-MSP) — single forward pass; uncertainty = 1 − max softmax probability. Zero inference overhead; used as the reference baseline.
  • MC Dropout — T = 30 stochastic forward passes with dropout active at inference time. Uncertainty from predictive entropy and mutual information.
  • Deep Ensemble — three independently fine-tuned checkpoints; predictions averaged. Uncertainty from inter-model disagreement.
  • Temperature Scaling — post-hoc calibration: a single scalar T fitted on validation data softens all confidence scores. Rank-equivalent to Confidence but better calibrated.
